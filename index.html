FAQ

Questions, answered

Does BizDash work offline? +

Yes. BizDash is desktop-first and works fully offline. Data syncs to the cloud when you're online — never required.

Which platforms are supported? +

Windows 10/11 today, with macOS and Linux builds rolling out soon. All builds ship from a single codebase.

Can I import data from my old POS? +

Absolutely. We support CSV imports out of the box and offer free migration help on Growth and Scale plans.

Is my data secure? +

Yes. Local data is encrypted at rest. Cloud sync uses end-to-end encryption with keys you control.

Do you offer a free trial? +

The Starter plan is free forever. Growth and Scale come with a 14-day no-card free trial.
